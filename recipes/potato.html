Potato Wedges

Description

These crispy potato wedges make for a great side dish.

Ingredients

  • Paprika(2T)
  • Olive oil(2T)
  • garlic powder(1T)
  • Chili powder(1T)
  • Salt(1t)
  • Black poper(1t)
  • 4 large potatoes, cut into wedges
  • 1 cup bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees C.

  2. Mix paprika, olive oil, garlic powder, chill powder, salt, and black peppper together in a bowl until marinade is smooth.

  3. Place potatoes in a baking dish; add marinade and rub into potatoes. Sprinkle bread crumbs over potatoes and coat each wedge well.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Reduce heat to 180 degrees and continue cooking until wedges are tender enough to puncture with a fork, anout 30 minutes more.
