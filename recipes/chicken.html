Beer Can Chicken

Description

This chicken is perfectly seasoned with herbs and spices and baked to a crispy pergection.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • garlic salt(2T)
  • paprika(2T)
  • 1 can beer
  • 1 whole chicken

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for low heat and lightly oil the grate

  2. Melt 1/2 cup butter in a small skillet.
