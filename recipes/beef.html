Beef Bourguignon

Description

Beef Bourguignon(a.k.a beef burgundy) is a French beef stew braised in red wine and beef broth.

Ingredients

  • Wine: Red Burgundy wine

  • Brandy

  • Fresh and canned vegetables: onions, carrots, mushrooms and tomato paste.

  • Olive oil

  • Spices and seasonings: parsley, a bay leaf, garlic, black peppercorns and salt.

  • butter

Steps
